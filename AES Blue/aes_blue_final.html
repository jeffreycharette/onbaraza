You're invited to Omidyar Network's second African Entrepreneurship Summit being held on 2 October in Belle Mare, Mauritius.

ON Baraza 2013
30 September - 1 October
Held exclusively for leaders of our Africa-based and Africa-focused investee organizations, ON Baraza is an opportunity to expand your individual and organizational success. Through connection, cohort relationships and shared learning, you'll refine and build key business and leadership skills and have a chance to test pre-market innovation ideas.

African Entrepreneurship Summit
2 October
We're bringing together leaders from diverse regions, sectors and backgrounds to share and learn about effective solutions for strengthening local entrepreneurship ecosystems. Building on the recommendations set forth in the Accelerating Entrepreneurship in Africa report, issued by Omidyar Network in collaboration with Monitor Group, the Summit is designed to move from insights to action. Through panel discussions and interactive sessions, our goal is to enable the understanding and application of practical approaches to advancing Africa's entrepreneur economy so that we, together, can drive greater prosperity.
