You're invited to Omidyar Network's second African Entrepreneurship Summit being held on 2 October in Belle Mare, Mauritius.

This exclusive event brings together leaders from diverse regions, sectors and backgrounds to share and learn about effective solutions for strengthening local entrepreneurship ecosystems.

Building on the recommendations set forth in the Accelerating Entrepreneurship in Africa report, issued by Omidyar Network in collaboration with Monitor Group, the Summit is designed to move from insights to action. Through panel discussions and interactive sessions, our goal is to enable the understanding and application of practical approaches to advancing Africa's entrepreneur economy so that we, together, can drive greater prosperity; this is a theme that Omidyar Network shares closely with our partners and friends from the African Leadership Network who will be joining us.

Please visit the event website to find out more and to register.

We hope you'll join us on 2 October!

Malik Fal
Managing Director
Omidyar Network Africa
